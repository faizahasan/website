   GENERAL SECRETARY MESSAGE

Hindustan Agricultural Research & Welfare society, the most prestigious registered society under the Societies Act 1860, of plasticizing Agriculturist from public and private institutions and the students pursuing Agriculture disciplines, has vital role to play in fostering the progress  of Agriculture education in the country. Education is the basis of all progress. It is for this very reason that we forayed into education. Over a decade of experience has taught us that progress is possible only, if men and women are equally well-educated. Presently our Society works with Agriculture Institution in India to reduce poverty, provide education and has made many innovative ideas to facilitate a good agriculture, socio-economic and environment development.
